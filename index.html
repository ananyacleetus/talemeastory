Tale Me A Story

Everyone has a story!

Tale me a Story is a non-profit organisation that connects elderly citizens with middle school students. It promotes civic engagement amongst the elderly. Students aproach senior citizens in their community as a part of an after-school initiative. They record the elderly person's or 'storyteller's', as we like to call them, experiences and wisdom. This is done over the course of two months. It providees an opportunity to the students to learn journalistic skills. Moreover, it gives the senior citizens a chance to do what they love the most - tell stories!
